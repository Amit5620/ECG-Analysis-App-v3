Why is ECG Analysis Important?

ECG analysis is crucial for early detection of heart diseases. It helps identify irregular heart rhythms, heart attacks, and other cardiac conditions. By monitoring the electrical activity of the heart, healthcare professionals can make informed decisions about treatment and management, ultimately improving patient outcomes.

How Does Our Website Help?

Our website utilizes advanced AI algorithms to analyze ECG data efficiently. Users can upload their ECG recordings, and our models will detect abnormalities and provide insights into potential heart conditions. This empowers users to take proactive steps towards their heart health, with easy access to analysis and recommendations.
